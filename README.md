# sensAI

> **AI-driven Interview Preparation and Industry Insights Platform**

## Overview

**sensAI** is an intelligent platform designed to support users in preparing for interviews and staying informed about their preferred industry. Using advanced Artificial Intelligence, sensAI fetches pertinent data, curates resources, and tailors guidance and practice questions for users based on their chosen career field. The system ensures a personalized preparation experience, enhancing users’ confidence and readiness for real-world interviews.

## Features

- **Industry Insights:** Receive up-to-date information, trends, and news about your selected industry, curated by AI.
- **Interview Preparation:** Access AI-generated interview questions, best practices, and guides specific to your domain.
- **Personalized Experience:** Let sensAI adapt recommendations and content to your background and career goals.
- **Data-Driven Guidance:** All insights, suggestions, and resources are fetched and processed by AI, ensuring relevance and accuracy.
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor your preparation journey and track areas for improvement.
- **Customizable Settings:** Select your target industry and personalize interview scenarios.
